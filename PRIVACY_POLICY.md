# Privacy Policy

**Jing Tracker**

*Last updated: March 3, 2026*

## Overview

Jing Tracker is a sexual wellness tracking app that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is fundamental to the design of this app.

## Data Collection

Jing Tracker does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer, third parties, or external servers. All data you enter into the app remains exclusively on your device.

## Data Storage

All wellness event data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not sent to the developer or any third party.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your app data may be included in your iCloud backup as part of Apple's standard backup process. This is managed entirely by Apple and governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). You can manage iCloud Backup in your device's Settings. Jing Tracker does not independently sync or transmit data to iCloud or any other cloud service.

## Third-Party Services

Jing Tracker does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data processing services.

## Network Access

Jing Tracker does not independently connect to the internet. The only network activity that may occur is through Apple's iCloud Backup, which is controlled by your device settings.

## Your Control

You have full control over your data at all times. You can export, import, or delete all of your data directly within the app's Settings screen.

## Children's Privacy

Jing Tracker is not directed at children under 17.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ashtacore/jing--tracker/issues).
